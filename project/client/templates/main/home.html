Food you eat impacts the environment

It's hard to understand the environmental impact of everyday foods you eat. Where do you even start?

We'll start by looking at ecological costs from transportation and water.


For consumers in the United States, our food comes from all over the world.

What is a water footprint?

A water footprint breaks down the types of water inputs for growing food.

There are different footprints to make up the total water footprint for a crop.

Blue

Green

Grey

Total


  • Blue: Surface water & groundwater inputs
  • Green: Rainwater inputs
  • Grey: Volume of freshwater to balance out pollutants from crop production
